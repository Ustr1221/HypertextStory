My Computer Science Journey: First Experience With Computers
Just before I started kindergarten, my parents bought a computer. It was still
our first year here in the U.S., and my parents did not really know how to
use it, so it was all up to my older brother to figure it out and explain it to them.
I did not have much interest at first until my brother showed me some games
that I could play. Besides watching YouTube and playing games, I did
not have any interest in computers.
