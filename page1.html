My Computer Science Journey: First Time Seeing HTML
In middle school, I got my first school laptop and one of my friends showed me
how to change webpages for myself using the 'inspect' feature. I played around
with it by doing little things like changing the google logo to something else,
or pranking my younger brother into thinking I was in a video with his favorite
youtuber. I also used it to change my grade when showing it to my parents
in the middle of quarters since my grades were not that good until the end of
the quarter when I turn in every late assignment in the final week.
